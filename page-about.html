About KID Friendly

KID Friendly is a 100% voluntary, privately-run organisation designed with the purpose of educating parents and children about the internet and creating awareness for the dangers and important key points associated with the online world.

What we do

We cover a wide range of topics including cyber bullying, current app trends among children and teens, helpful tips and advice for parents and key pieces of information for children including stranger danger and defining the internet.

You, as the parent, can create an activity for your child in the 'Create Activity' section found on the dashboard. You child can then complete a series of educational activities, under your guidance, in the 'Activities' section.
