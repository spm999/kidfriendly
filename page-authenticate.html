Join Friendly

Hello there! Are you a parent/guardian introducing your child to the internet for the first time, and you're concerned about your child's safety on the internet? If so, why don't you come join Netty on a quest to complete activities and prepare you and your child with helpful tips and advice?

Once you join, you will be introduced to the Parent Dashboard. Here you can create personalised activities for your child as well as view your child's Progress.

Your child can complete a series of educational activities such as "Stranger Danger", which provides information about the ways some people may take advantage of the internet and your child can choose other activities too! All can be found in the Activities section.
